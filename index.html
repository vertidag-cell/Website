FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Quick's ARK Bot?
Quick's ARK Bot is a premium Discord automation bot built for ARK PvP servers, hosting communities, and gaming networks. It handles tickets, payments, staff pay, moderation, and player engagement systems in one place.
Do I need coding knowledge?
No. The bot is configured entirely through a Discord button-driven setup hub. No commands, no config files, no coding required.
How do I subscribe?
Invite the bot, then run /subscribe in your server and complete the PayPal payment. Your server is activated automatically after payment is confirmed.
Does it work for multiple Discord servers?
Yes. The bot is fully multi-guild. Each server has its own configuration, payments, and staff records.
Can I cancel?
Yes. Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time through your PayPal account. Your access remains active until the end of the current billing period.
Does PayPal activate the bot automatically?
Yes. PayPal webhooks confirm payment instantly and your server is activated without manual approval.
Can I use my own PayPal for my server's payments?
Yes. The bot supports per-server PayPal configuration so the payments your players make go directly to your PayPal account.
Is ARK Guard / Nitrado included?
ARK Guard and Nitrado integration are on the roadmap and marked as Coming Soon. They are not currently active features.
How do I get support?
Join the support Discord using the link in the navbar, or contact the owner directly through Discord or email.
