Demo guide

What each walkthrough shows

Click a card to load that demo in the player above.

Free

/setup — Setup Hub

Every module configured from one button-driven hub. The demo walks tickets setup end-to-end: forum channel, staff role, auto-routing — about five minutes in real life.

Premium

/ark — Cluster Operations

The ARK hub panel: players, controls, bans, wipes and logs over the Nitrado API. The demo runs a player search and pulls a full profile — sessions, tribe, name history, gamerscore, ban status.

Premium

ARK Guard — Anti-Cheat Review

A 14-signal review system. The demo shows an auto-alert, the evidence card behind it, and the staff decision — watchlist, ban, or clear. Flags are evidence for humans, never auto-bans.

Premium

Live Logs + Chat Relay

In-game chat relayed into Discord as it happens, join/kill/tribe events posted live, and everything organized into clean, searchable forum posts — no channel spam.

Premium

One-Tap Rollback

Pick a Nitrado backup, confirm the one destructive step, restored. The demo shows the full flow including the confirmation gate and the rollback landing in your history.

Free

/pop — Cluster Population

The whole cluster in one embed — per-map players, online status, and auto-drawn 24h charts. Free on every server, forever.

Premium

/subscribe — Activation

PayPal checkout in Discord with webhook auto-confirmation — the demo shows premium activating the moment payment lands. $15 per 30 days, cancel anytime.

Premium

Premium Branding

Default bot → your brand: name, accent color, logo and footer morph across embeds, panels, /pop boards, tickets and welcome messages.

Premium

Tickets & Payments

A ticket opens, staff sells a kit with an in-ticket payment link, and the embed flips to PAID when the webhook confirms — plus staff pay logging the commission.

Premium

Hype Rewards

Players earn expiring credits for name tags, invites and boosts — the demo shows a reward firing and landing in a player's balance.

Free + Premium

Pet System

Free feeding, renaming and inventory — and the premium layer: rarity tiers, boss fights, fusion and leaderboards.

Cheat sheet

The commands behind the demos

/ark — cluster operations: players, controls, bans, wipes, logs (premium)

/subscribe — activate premium for the server, in Discord

/grace — post-wipe protection timers for fresh servers (premium)

/leaderboard · /rank — XP & activity boards (free)
