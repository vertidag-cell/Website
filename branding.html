New embeds, panels, /pop boards, tickets and welcome messages use your brand immediately. Switch back anytime.

Common questions

Branding, honestly answered

Does the bot's name actually change in my server? +
Yes — premium branding sets the bot's nickname in your server (up to 32 characters), plus a per-server avatar and banner. The underlying Discord application stays "Arkoris", so the @mention handle is unchanged.
Is this a white-label / separate bot? +
No. Branding customizes how Arkoris looks inside your server — it does not create a separate Discord bot application for your guild. A dedicated white-label offering may come later, separately.
Can different servers have different brands? +
Yes. Branding is scoped per Discord server — every premium guild configures its own name, colors, logo and footer independently.
What happens if my premium expires? +
The bot falls back to the default Arkoris look. Your saved branding is kept, so renewing brings it back without re-entering anything.
