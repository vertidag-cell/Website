What Gets Branded

Every Bot Surface, Customized

Brand name, accent color, logo, and footer text apply consistently across the surfaces your members see.

Embed Color & Title

Set a custom accent color across every bot embed. Override the "Arkoris" prefix in titles.

/pop Embed Title

Show "Iron ARK Cluster Population" instead of "ARK Cluster Population".

Ticket Panel

Brand your ticket open panel with custom title, description, and embed color.

Payment Panel

Customize the subscribe panel title and footer so it feels native to your community.

Welcome Embed

Welcome new members with your brand colors, logo, and footer.

Charts & Panels

Population charts and other dashboard panels use your accent color.

Footer Attribution

Optional footer line crediting your brand. "Powered by Arkoris" stays.

Per-Server, Per-Guild

Branding is scoped per Discord server. Different guilds can have different brands.
